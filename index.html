About Restura Mika

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by the Yeager Family, Restura Mika has been serving authentic Japanese cuisine for over three decades. Our passion for traditional recipes and fresh ingredients has been made us a beloved destination for food lovers


Every dish is prepared with love and attention to detail, using recipes passed down througn generations of Japanese chefs.
